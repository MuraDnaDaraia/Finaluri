Formation

Rammstein was formed by an ensemble of musicians from East Germany in 1994. Their early work was heavily influenced by the industrial scene and the reunification of Germany, which brought a mix of cultural and musical influences.

Breakthrough

With the release of their second album, "Sehnsucht" in 1997, Rammstein achieved international fame, thanks in part to the hit single "Du Hast." Their unique blend of heavy metal and electronic music, combined with theatrical live shows, garnered a global fanbase.
